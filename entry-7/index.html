Sanctuary

Paragraph 1

               A world born out of love,

    Devolved into vile hatred.

A merciless disdain for their own kin,

Ravaging both cradles and graves.

                               Scorn and scorched earth,

               Filled with wanderers desperate for a form of rebirth.

                 Out of harm's way, the light of opportunity shines,

Carving a path, far away into the heavens.

Unknowing, and all knowing,

The survivors yearn for the coddling arms of their destination,

    To be absolved from guilt,

            To be humans rebuilt, born from the ashes of their nation.
