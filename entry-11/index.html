Memoriam Locket

Paragraph 1

Head over heels you fell for me,

Bubble gum pops endlessly.

Gems glisten, sweet tooth so so sweet,

A taste of ecstasy.

Bliss of you, carefree.

I miss you, I miss me.

Paragraph 2

Downwing in the sea of struggle,

The bubbles pop,

The luster of gemstones dull.

Set me free, Aphrodite.

The fantasy that you can’t see.

Just wait for me.

I'll love you, even if your shine remains an absentee.
