Distance Between Us

Paragraph 1

Twinkling, glistening, courting, light.

Flickers fleeting, dissipating over the expanse,

An enamoring entrance between two stars.

One light reaching out to the other,

Ever inviting, but never anything more.

The vapid gap tearing a hole within the pair,

Dimming together, as destiny awaits.

Paragraph 2

A window into what have could been,

A glimpse into a life never lived,

A window into what could have been.

Spinning, turning, in the space between us,

I wait for destiny’s sign,

I catch a glimpse of you, shining in the dark,

Spinning, shining, seperating,

Forever.
