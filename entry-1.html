Seperation Entry #1 Apart of All

Awakening in familiarity, the scene’s masquerade tells of secrets

Inviting with a beckoning finger, comfort does not open her arms

She walks forwards, looking back occasionally for the hopeful

Her shaul sweeping the ground beneath her strides

The distance insights liberation, and liberation, heartache

Ideation of the self

Slips with each step taken

A wish of intimacy

A dream of familiarity
