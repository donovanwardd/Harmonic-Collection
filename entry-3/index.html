Circle

Paragraph 1

A landscape,

Detailed with years of attention,

Fades and fades and fades.

A sliver of life,

An entire world past,

Seems like nothing at all.

Sameness and sorrow,

Legitimate but fleeting,

Reminds the soul of the life it was once leading.

Seeing the cavern,

Regonzing the rift,

I wake up again and again and again,

Reminiscing about all that has been missed.
